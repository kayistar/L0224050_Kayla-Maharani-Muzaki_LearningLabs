NCT DREAM

엔시티 드림

NCT DREAM (엔씨티 DREAM) is the 3rd sub-unit of the boy group NCT under SM Entertainment. The sub-unit consists of 7 members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Chenle, Jaemin, and Jisung. They debuted on August 25, 2016, with their first single, Chewing Gum. They made their Japanese debut on February 8, 2023, with their first single, Best Friend Ever.
